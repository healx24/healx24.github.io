WHAT IS HEALX24

Healx24 is a medical services providing platform which is trying to create medical services free and cheaper for students in private hospitals by providing them a Healx24 card through this card students get access to our partner hospitals where they get Free doctor consultation, 20% on medicines and lab test and the most important Free treatment in any accidental case etc.
